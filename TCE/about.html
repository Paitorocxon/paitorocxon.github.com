About Toecramp.





What exactly is Toecramp?

Have you ever heard somebody say that something is impossible and thought, 'what if it is possible'?
That's exactly what I had in mind when starting the adventurous endeavour of developing a complex engine in, with and for Visual Basic.Net.
One of the big goals is, to deliver beginners a good, not too complex but still powerful "game engine".

On this site, I will keep you updated on the process of this visionary project. Feel free to dive into my documentation!
